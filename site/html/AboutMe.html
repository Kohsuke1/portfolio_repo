About Me

I enjoy sketching, free drawing, painting, 3D modeling and coding. My workevolves around animation, movie films and still picture graphic or hand drawn art. To create a piece of work that emotionally moves people, instilling a sense of wonder and imagination to the viewer.
In my last two years of high school, I studied animation and gaming design at the Academy of Interactive Entertainment (AIE), where I acquired and developed various artistic, software and design skills. I learned different 2D animation software programs, including Flipbook, ToonBoom, Autodesk Maya as the 3D modeling and animation software, to creating games (as well as learning to code) in Unity game engine.
Presently enrolled at the University of Washington Bothell to further my knowledge in the area of web media and technology. My studies includes a courses in computer programming using java and website and media designs.
